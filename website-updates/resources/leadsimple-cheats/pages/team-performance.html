Understanding the Metrics

Tasks Due Tasks with a due-date of Today
Tasks Overdue Tasks with a due-date before today
Future Tasks Tasks with a due-date after today
Task Completion The % of tasks where the teammate hit "complete" (not "skip")
Tasks Skipped The % of tasks where the teammate hit "skip" (not "complete")

Diagnostic Questions

When reviewing your Team Performance data, use these questions to identify issues:

Questions to Ask:

  • Which teammates have the most overdue tasks?
  • Which teammates have the most future tasks?
  • Which teammates have the highest % of tasks Skipped?

üí° Important Context

üìù Note About BDMs & Sales Staff

Your BDM or Sales-person will likely have a very high amount of future tasks, and skipped tasks.

While this can be avoided with process design, it's likely going to be a tension to manage rather than a problem to solve. Sales processes are inherently less linear and more dependent on external factors.

What High Skip Rates Can Indicate:

  • Process issues: Tasks that aren't applicable to every situation
  • Training gaps: Team members not understanding task purpose
  • Workflow problems: Tasks that are redundant or poorly timed
  • Role misalignment: Tasks assigned to wrong team members

What High Overdue Rates Can Indicate:

  • Capacity issues: Too much work for the team member
  • Prioritization problems: Unclear on what matters most
  • Due date issues: Unrealistic timeframes set in processes
  • External blockers: Waiting on others to complete their part
