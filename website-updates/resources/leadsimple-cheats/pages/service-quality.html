üí° Analysis: Combining These Metrics

Understanding how these two metrics relate to each other is key to diagnosing issues:

üìä Processes On Time = LOW + Task Completion = HIGH

Your team is likely doing the work required, but you need to adjust default due dates. They're completing tasks but running out of time on the overall process.

üìä Processes On Time = HIGH + Task Completion = LOW

Your processes likely have tasks and stages that are not helpful or applicable, or not timed properly. Consider auditing which tasks are being skipped.

üìä Task Completion = 100%

Your team probably doesn't know the "skip" button exists, and may be completing tasks but not actually doing the work. Train your team on proper task handling.

üìä Both Metrics = HIGH but still having issues?

You're dealing with a training issue, not a process issue. Your workflows are sound - focus on team enablement.
