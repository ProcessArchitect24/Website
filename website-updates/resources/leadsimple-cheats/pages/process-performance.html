üìù Rule of Thumb

35-40% of your total door count should be in an active process at all times. If that number is higher, it may indicate that you have processes that are stuck, old, or backlogged.

Diagnostic Questions

When reviewing your Process Performance data, ask yourself:

Questions to Ask:

  • Which processes have the most overdue?
  • Which processes have the most off-track tasks?
  • Which processes have the lowest Task Completion Rate?
  • Which processes have the lowest % Completed on Time?
